About Us


Montante Plastic Surgery and Aesthetics is the culmination of multiple talents that have come together to provide comprehensive aesthetic and reconstructive surgery of the face, breast, and body. We also provide state of the art non-surgical aesthetic treatments. We strive to tailor each patients entire experience with our practice to meet their individual needs. Our mission is to provide aesthetic FORM and function, maximal BEAUTY through invasive and non-invasive techniques, all PERSONALIZED to meet the unique goals of each individual patient.

The Team


Steven J. Montante, MD: Dr. Montante is a board certified plastic surgeon with 14 years of practice experience. Following his medical education at the University of Pittsburgh, he completed residency training in Plastic Surgery at the Medical College of Virginia at Virginia Commonwealth University. His robust and varied practice experience has included being an Assistant Professor of Plastic Surgery at VCU School of Medicine. He currently serves our country’s veterans as a part-time staff surgeon at the Richmond McGuire VA Medical Center.

Shelly R. Montante, MSN, LNP: Shelly is a licensed Nurse Practitioner with 10 years of nursing experience. After careers as an international fashion model and gallery owner, she pursued a career in nursing. After obtaining her BSN from Virginia Commonwealth University, she matriculated into the operating room and achieved her Certified Nurses of the Operating Room. She continued to further her education by becoming a Registered Nurse First Assist, with particular expertise in robotics and plastic surgery. She achieved her Masters of Nursing in Adult Gerontology Primary Care from Walden University. In addition to her formal education, Shelly is a certified Nurse Injector for a variety of injectable treatments, and is a licensed provider of medical and aesthetic tattooing.



Elle Layman, MBA: Elle Layman joins Montante Plastic Surgery & Aesthetics from The Brink's Company, where she was the Corporate Strategy Analyst for Mexico, Brazil, France, Canada and the U.S. Elle received her Masters of Business Administration from Virginia Commonwealth University in 2014. Her specialties include communications, business administration and data analytics, and she has a passion for using technology to improve and simplify business processes.
